Onki.fi

”Onki.fi is a service that contains Finnish and international (fi, se, en) ontologies, vocabularies and thesauri needed for publishing content cost-efficiently to the Semantic Web.”

Onki.fi

•  Service is maintained by the Semantic Computing Research Group in the Aalto University.
•  It contains several different ontologies that can be used either separately or all at the same time.

Onki module


During the development of SYND we've build up a module that integrates the ontologies served by Onki.fi with Drupal taxonomies.
Credits to Tomi Mikola, Mearra

Onki module

We wanted to have a semantic solution that would get us a better searchability and findality but also a solution that would support the editors in the content creating process.

Arpa service

•  Arpa is a subfunctionality of Onki which automates the tagging process.
•  Arpa takes in content and tries to guess what tags would best describe the content.
•  We've also integrated our system with ARPA (coming also as a contrib module).

Onki & Arpa
- obstacles

•  Downtime issues with Onki.fi servers
•  No possibility to add custom terms (yet!)
•  Some categories are not available (spatial, tv-programs, people...etc.)

Onki & Arpa
- benefits

•  Users: Better searchability
•  Editors: Easy way to tag content
•  Business: Better visibility
•  Developers: Easier access
